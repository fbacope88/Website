Most businesses assume getting a professional website built takes weeks. Briefing sessions, revisions, back-and-forth emails, waiting around. It doesn't have to be that way.

## Why Speed Matters More Than Ever

The business landscape moves fast. A new competitor can launch online overnight. A seasonal campaign needs to go live this week, not next month. A business rebrand needs to be reflected immediately.

When your brand's online presence is stuck behind a slow agency timeline, you're losing:

- **Revenue** from customers who can't find you

Speed, when paired with quality, is a competitive advantage.

## What Happens in Those 72 Hours?

### Hour 0–6: Brief & Strategy

We start by understanding your business, your customers, and your goals. What pages do you need? What's your brand aesthetic? What action do you want visitors to take?

This isn't a 20-question form — it's a focused conversation that gives us everything we need to build something that actually works for your business.

### Hour 6–36: Design & Build

Using proven design systems and frameworks, we build your website at speed — but with precision. Every section is purposefully designed to:

- Build trust immediately
- Guide visitors towards your desired action
- Load fast on all devices
- Look stunning on mobile, tablet, and desktop

### Hour 36–60: SEO Setup & Testing

A fast website is worthless if Google can't find it. We implement:

- Proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3 structure)
- Meta titles and descriptions for every page
- Schema markup for rich search results
- Fast page load speeds (optimised images, clean code)
- Mobile responsiveness checks across devices

### Hour 60–72: Review & Launch

You review the finished site, we make any final tweaks, and it goes live. Simple.
